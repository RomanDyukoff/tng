    PERSONAL INFORMATION OF USERS WHICH IS RECEIVED AND PROCESSED BY THE RESTAURANT

    Personal data includes all information or data that identifies an individual or can be used to identify an individual, and also relates to such information or data. Information is provided by the user at their discretion.

    In any case, personal data includes:

    • Last name, first name, patronymic of the user;

    • email address (E-MAIL);

    The Restaurant generally does not verify the accuracy of the personal information provided by users and does not exercise control over their legal capacity. However, the Restaurant assumes that the information provided to it by users is accurate and current.

    The restaurant collects statistics about the IP addresses of its visitors. This information is used to identify and resolve technical problems.

    Any other personal information not specified above (browsers and operating systems used, etc.) is subject to secure storage and non-distribution, except as provided for in this Policy.

    Such data as telephone number and email address cannot be classified as personal data of the user if the processing of this data is carried out impersonally, without reference to the personal data of a particular user, their order history in the Restaurant.

    COLLECTION (RECEPTION) OF PERSONAL DATA

    The processing of personal data in this Policy means: collection of the above data, their systematization, accumulation, storage, clarification (updating, changing), use, blocking, destruction.

    The restaurant stores personal information of users in accordance with these rules, ensuring the security of user personal data from unauthorized transfer to third parties.

    The restaurant takes the necessary organizational and technical measures to protect personal data. The measures taken are based on the requirements of Art. 18.1, Article 19 of the Federal Law of July 27, 2006 No. 152-FZ “On Personal Data”.
