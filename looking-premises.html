    Premises requirements

    Tangiers Lounge can be located on any floor in a separate building (except for the basement), in a non-residential building.

    1
    Any floor

    The total area of ​​the premises is 170-300 sq.m.

    2
    Room area

    It is necessary to have technical specifications for the ventilation system or the possibility of its placement on the facade of the building or in the house shaft.

    3
    Ventilation system

    Technical specifications for power supply are required. Minimum power - 70 kW.

    4
    Electricity supply

    It is necessary to have technical conditions for the sewerage and drainage system of the premises.

    5
    Sewerage and drainage
